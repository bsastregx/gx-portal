Thank you for choosing GeneXus Server as your team development platform. This message contains information about the setting up of your GeneXus Server space.

As a subscriber of the GeneXus Server service, you are entitled to a custom URL in the genexusserver.com domain for your space. Your URL will have the following format:

https://[subdomain].genexusserver.com/[SpaceName]

You can choose both a subdomain and a SpaceName of your liking, although they will be subject to availability. For example, if you choose “acme” as your subdomain, and “development” as your space name, your URL will be as follows:

https://acme.genexusserver.com/development

Please reply to this message telling us the names you desire as soon as possible, so that we can start with the setup of your space. Once we receive this information, it may take up to 24 hours for your space to become active.
